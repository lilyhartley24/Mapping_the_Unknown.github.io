Interactive Heatmap: UFO Sightings by Day of Week and Time of Day

Data source
This visualization uses scrubbed.csv and organizes sightings by day of week and time of day.
Interaction
Hover over any cell to view the exact day, time, and number of sightings for that combination.

Takeaway

This visualization shows that UFO sightings are not evenly distributed across the week or throughout the day. Reports are much more concentrated during evening and late-night hours, especially around the end of the week and on weekends. In contrast, early morning and midday periods tend to show substantially lower activity. This pattern suggests that the timing of UFO sightings may be influenced not only by when unusual aerial events are noticed, but also by when people are most likely to be outside, looking at the sky, or reporting what they see.
