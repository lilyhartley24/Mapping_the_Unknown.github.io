Interactive Bar Chart: Top 10 UFO Shapes in U.S. Sightings (2005 Onward)

Data source
This visualization uses scrubbed.csv, filtered to UFO sightings in the us only.
How the data was prepared
The chart focuses on sightings from 2005 onward. The shape field was cleaned by lowercasing values and removing blank, missing, unknown, and other categories before counting frequencies.
Interaction
Hover over a bar to highlight it and show its count. Use this to quickly compare UFO shape categories.

Takeaway

This visualization shows that a small number of UFO shapes account for a large share of reported sightings in the United States since 2005. In particular, light, circle, triangle, and fireball appear far more frequently than the remaining categories, suggesting that reported sightings tend to cluster around a few recurring visual descriptions rather than being evenly distributed across many shapes. This pattern may reflect both the kinds of objects witnesses most commonly believe they observe and the limited vocabulary people use when describing unfamiliar aerial events.
